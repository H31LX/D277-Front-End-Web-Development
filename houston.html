Welcome to Houston, Texas!

Known as the "Space City," Houston is a dynamic and diverse metropolis with a rich cultural scene, world-class museums, and a thriving energy industry. The city is famous for its warm hospitality, delicious cuisine, and vibrant neighborhoods. Whether you're visiting for business or pleasure, Houston offers endless opportunities to explore and enjoy.

Houston Highlights

  • Largest city in Texas
  • Key hub for energy and space industries
  • Diverse cultural communities and festivals

Top 3 Things to Experience in Houston

  1. Explore the Space Center Houston.
  2. Visit the Houston Museum District.
  3. Enjoy diverse culinary experiences across the city.

Houston at a Glance

Houston, Texas, has a population of approximately 2.3 million residents as of 2023[1]. The city boasts a diverse community and a strong economy driven by energy, healthcare, and aerospace industries. The median household income in Houston is around $63,000, reflecting its broad job market[2].

Houston’s climate is classified as humid subtropical, with hot summers averaging highs near 94°F (34°C) and mild winters where freezing temperatures are rare[3]. The city experiences high humidity and frequent sunshine, making outdoor activities popular year-round.
